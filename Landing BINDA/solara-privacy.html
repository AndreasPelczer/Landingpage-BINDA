Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

SOLARA does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is not just respected — it is irrelevant to us, because we have nothing to do with your data.

Data Collection

SOLARA collects no personal information. No names, no emails, no location data, no usage analytics, no device identifiers. Nothing. The cosmos does not need your data.

Local Storage

All task data you create in SOLARA is stored locally on your device. It never leaves your phone. We cannot see it, access it, or read your cosmic task states.

In-App Purchases

SOLARA offers optional in-app purchases (such as the Virtual Healing Stone). These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Third-Party Services

SOLARA does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs, no Firebase, no Facebook pixels, no anything. Just you and the cosmos.

Children's Privacy

SOLARA does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (unlikely, since we collect nothing), updates will be posted on this page.
